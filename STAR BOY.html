STAR BOY


  "Starboy" is the third studio album by Canadian artist the Weeknd, released on November 25, 2016. 
  It was supported by eight singles, two of which topped the US Billboard Hot 100. The album received favorable reviews 
  and debuted at number one on the US Billboard 200 and the Canadian Albums Chart. It won Best Urban Contemporary 
  Album at the 60th Annual Grammy Awards in 2018. As of April 2022, it is certified four-times platinum by the RIAA.
  In 2023, a deluxe version was released with three additional remixed releases.
  
  Songwriter(s):- Abel Tesfaye, Guy-Manuel de Homem-Christo, Thomas Bangalter, Martin McKinney, Henry Walter.
  Producer(s):- Daft Punk, Don McKinney Cirkut, The Weeknd.
