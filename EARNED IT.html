EARNED IT


 "Earned It" is a song by the Weeknd, released as the lead single from the Fifty Shades of Grey soundtrack and
  included on his album, Beauty Behind the Madness. The song peaked at number three on the Billboard Hot 100, 
  becoming the Weeknd's first top five single.The song was nominated for Best Original Song at the 88th Academy 
  Awards, won the Grammy for Best R&B Performance, and was certified diamond by the RIAA in 2023 for selling 
  over ten million copies in the U.S.

  Songwriter(s):- Stephan Richard Moccio, Jason Quenneville, Ahmad Balshe, Abel Tesfaye.
  Producer(s):- Stephan Moccio, Jason Quenneville.
