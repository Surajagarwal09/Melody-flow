LOSE YOU TO LOVE ME


  "Lose You to Love Me" is a pop ballad by **Selena Gomez**, released as the lead single from her third studio album,Rare, 
  on October 23, 2019. The song, written by Gomez, Julia Michaels, Justin Tranter, and its producers Mattman & Robin, is about 
  self-discovery and empowerment. It topped the Billboard Hot 100, marking Gomez's first number one song on the chart, and also 
  reached number one in Canada and the Republic of Ireland. It was well-received by critics and has earned several gold, platinum, 
  and multi-platinum certifications worldwide. The song was listed as one of the best songs of 2019 by Vulture and Billboard. 

  Songwriter(s):- Selena Gomez, Julia Michaels, Justin Trante, Mattias Larsson, Robin Fredriksson.
  Producer(s):- Mattman & Robin.
