SELENA GOMEZ


Selena Marie Gomez


 Selena Gomez is an American singer, songwriter, actress, and businesswoman. She gained fame as a child actress on
 the TV show Barney & Friends and later starred in Wizards of Waverly Place. As a solo artist, she topped the charts
 with albums like Revival and Rare. Her hit singles include “Lose You to Love Me” and “Good for You”. Gomez has acted
 in films, produced TV shows. Beyond entertainment, she advocates for mental health and equality. She founded the 
 cosmetics brand Rare Beauty and is a social media powerhouse. Gomez has received numerous accolades,including 
 an American Music Award, a Billboard Music Award, two MTV Video Music Awards, and 16 Guinness World Records; 
 and received nominations for four Emmy Awards, two Grammy Awards, and two Golden Globe Awards.
