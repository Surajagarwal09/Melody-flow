THE MONSTER


  "The Monster" is a song from Eminem's album, The Marshall Mathers LP 2 (2013), featuring Rihanna. The song, written by 
  multiple artists including Eminem and Rihanna, was released as the fourth single from the album on October 29, 2013. 
  It marks the fourth collaboration between the two artists. The lyrics reflect Eminem's thoughts on the downsides of fame and 
  Rihanna dealing with her inner struggles. The song received positive reviews and topped charts in twelve countries, 
  including the US Billboard Hot 100. It also marked Eminem's first number one on the US Hot R&B/Hip-Hop Songs chart.
  
  Songwriter(s):- Bebe Rexha, Marshall Mathers, Robyn Fenty, Aaron Kleinstub, Jonathan Bellion, Bryan Fryzel, Maki.
  Producer(s):- Frequency.
