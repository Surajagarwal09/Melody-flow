WOLVES


  "Wolves" is a song by **Selena Gomez** and music producer **Marshmello**, released by Interscope Records on 
  October 25, 2017. The song, co-written by Gomez, Ali Tamposi, Louis Bell, Brian Lee, Marshmello, and 
  Andrew Watt, topped the charts in Latvia, Poland, and Serbia, and reached the top 10 in 23 other countries. It was 
  the second-most successful electronic song of 2018 in the US and has been certified Platinum or higher in fifteen 
  countries, including Diamond in Brazil. 
  
  Songwriter(s):- Selena Gomez, Christopher Comstock, Andrew Watt, Ali Tamposi, Brian Lee, Louis Bell.
  Producer(s):- Marshmello, Andrew Watt.
