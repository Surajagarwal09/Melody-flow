HUMBLE


 "HUMBLE." is a song by American rapper Kendrick Lamar, released on March 30, 2017, with its music video. 
  The song was written by Lamar and producers Mike Will Made It and Pluss. The lyrics emphasize humility. 
  It was the lead single from Lamar's fourth studio album, "Damn." and became his second number-one single on the U
  Billboard Hot 100. At the 60th Annual Grammy Awards, the song received four nominations and 
  won in three categories: Best Rap Performance, Best Rap Song, and Best Music Video.

  Songwriter(s):- Kendrick Duckworth, Michael Williams II, Asheton Hogan.
  Producer(s):- Mike Will Made It, Pluss.
