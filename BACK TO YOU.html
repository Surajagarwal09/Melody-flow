BACK TO YOU


  "Back to You" is a song by Selena Gomez, featured on the soundtrack of the second season of the TV show
  "13 Reasons Why" and as a bonus track on her album "Rare". The song was released on May 10, 2018, following
  Billie Eilish and Khalid's "Lovely". It was commercially successful, reaching the top five in several countries including 
  Australia, Canada, and the Czech Republic, and the top 20 in the US, UK, and other countries. It marked Gomez's 15th 
  consecutive entry in the top 40 of the Billboard Hot 100, where it peaked at number 18. 
  
  Songwriter(s):- Parrish Warrington, Diederik Van Elsas, Amy Allen, Micah Premnath, Selena Gomez.
  Producer(s):- Trackside, Ian Kirkpatrick.
