IT AIN'T ME


  "It Ain't Me" is a song by DJ Kygo and singer Selena Gomez, released in 2017. It was the lead single from Kygo's 
  debut EP, Stargazing, and also appears on Gomez's album, Rare. The song, written by Kygo, Gomez, Brian Lee,
  Ali Tamposi, and Andrew Watt, is an EDM, dance-pop, electropop, and tropical house track. It narrates a past relationship
  ruined by alcoholism. The song achieved significant chart success, reaching number one in several countries and
  the top five in many others. A remix of the song went viral on TikTok in 2021.
