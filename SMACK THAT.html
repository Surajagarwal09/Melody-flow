SMACK THAT


  "Smack That" is a hit song by Akon featuring Eminem from Akon's second album, Konvicted (2006). The song was written 
  by both artists along with Mike Strange and Luis Resto, and produced by Eminem. The collaboration occurred after a 
  recording session for Shady Records rapper Obie Trice. The song was well-received by critics and was released as the first 
  single from Konvicted on September 26, 2006. It topped 10 charts, peaked at number two on the Billboard Hot 100, and sold 
  over three million downloads in the U.S. The song also earned a Grammy nomination for Best Rap/Sung Collaboration.
  
  Songwriter(s):- Aliaune Thiam, Marshall Mathers, Luis Resto, Mike Strange.
  Producer(s):- Eminem .
