KENDRICK LAMAR


Kendrick Lamar Duckworth 


 Kendrick Lamar, an influential American rapper and singer-songwriter, is celebrated as one of the greatest rapper.
 He received the Pulitzer Prize for Music, a rare honor for a non-classical or jazz musician.
 Lamar’s fusion of political critique and social commentary resonated with his generation.
 Notable albums: Good Kid, M.A.A.D City, To Pimp a Butterfly, and Damn.
 Chart-topping singles: “Humble,” “Swimming Pools (Drank),” and “Bitch, Don’t Kill My Vibe.”
 Awards: Grammys, MTV VMAs, and BET Hip Hop Awards.
 Beyond music, Lamar co-founded PGLang and advocates for racial equality and mental health awareness.
