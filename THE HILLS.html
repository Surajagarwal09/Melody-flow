THE HILLS


  "The Hills" is a song by the Weeknd, released in 2015 as the second single from his album, Beauty Behind the Madness.  
  It topped the Billboard Hot 100 in the U.S. for six weeks and became the Weeknd's first diamond-certified record, selling 
  over ten million copies. The song also reached number one in Canada and charted in the top ten in several other countries. 
  It has two official remixes featuring Eminem and Nicki Minaj.

  Songwriter(s):- Carlo Montagnese, Ahmad Balshe, Abel Tesfaye, Emmanuel Nickerson.
  Producer(s):- Illangelo, Mano.
