CANT'T FEEL MY FACE


  "Can't Feel My Face" is a song by the Weeknd from his album, Beauty Behind the Madness. Released in 2015,
  it was written by Max Martin, Peter Svensson, Ali Payami, Savan Kotecha, and the Weeknd. The song topped the 
  US Billboard Hot 100 and the Canadian Hot 100, and received a diamond certification from the RIAA for selling over 
  ten million copies. It was also nominated for two Grammy Awards. The song achieved number one in several other 
  countries and was a top 10 single in various territories.

  Songwriter(s):- Peter Anders Svensson, Ali Payami, Savan Harish Kotecha, Martin Max, Abel Tesfaye.
  Producer(s):- Max Martin, Ali Payami.
