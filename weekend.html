THE WEEKEND


Abel Makkonen Tesfaye


 Abel Makkonen Tesfaye, known as the Weeknd, is a Canadian singer-songwriter. He started his career in 2009 
 and co-founded the record label XO. He’s known for his unique style of R&B and has released several successful 
 albums including Kiss Land, Beauty Behind the Madness, Starboy, My Dear Melancholy, After Hours, and Dawn FM. 
 He also co-created and starred in the drama series The Idol. Tesfaye is one of the world’s best-selling music artists 
 with over 75 million records sold and holds several streaming and Billboard chart records.
 He was appointed a World Food Programme Goodwill Ambassador in 2021.
