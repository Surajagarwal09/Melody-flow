WE DON'T TALK ANYMORE


  "We Don't Talk Anymore" is a tropical-inspired pop song by **Charlie Puth** and **Selena Gomez**, released on May 24, 
  2016, as the third single from Puth's debut album, Nine Track Mind. The song, co-written with Jacob Kasher Hindlin, 
  peaked at number nine on the US Billboard Hot 100, marking Puth's second top-10 single and Gomez's sixth. It also 
  reached the top 10 in over 20 countries and topped the charts in Italy, Lebanon, and Romania. The song has received 
  Quintuple Platinum certification in the US, Australia, and Italy, and Diamond certification in France.

  Songwriter(s):- Charlie Puth, Selena Gomez, Jacob Kasher Hindlin.
  Producer(s):- Charlie Puth.
