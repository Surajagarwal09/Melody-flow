LOVE THE WAY YOU LIE


  "Lose Yourself" is a song by Eminem from the 2002 movie 8 Mile's soundtrack. It was Eminem's first Billboard Hot 100 
  number-one single, staying there for twelve weeks. The song, praised for its inspiring and aggressive themes, won the 
  Academy Award for Best Original Song and the Grammy Award for Best Rap Song and Best Rap Solo Performance. 
  It is also one of the three 21st-century rap songs included on Rolling Stone's list of the 500 Greatest Songs of All Time. 
  The song is certified 13Ã— Platinum and is the most streamed song on Spotify from the 2000s. Eminem performed it at the 
  92nd Academy Awards and the Super Bowl LVI halftime show. 
  
  Songwriter(s):- Eminem.
  Producer(s):- Eminem, Jeff Bass, Luis Resto.
