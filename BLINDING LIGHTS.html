BLINDING LIGHTS


  Blinding Lights” by the Weeknd was released in November 29, 2019 as the second single from his album 
  “After Hours”.  It achieved global success, topping charts in over 40 countries.
  Notably, it spent 90 weeks on the Billboard Hot 100, breaking records.
  The song was certified diamond in the United States and became the most streamed song on streaming platforms.
  It has official remixes and has been featured in video games like Fuser and NBA 2021.

  Songwriter(s):- Abel Tesfaye, Ahmad Balshe, Jason Quenneville, Max Martin, Oscar Holter.
  Producer(s):- Max MartinOscar Holter, The Weeknd.
