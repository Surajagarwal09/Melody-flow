LOVE


 "Love" is a song by American rapper Kendrick Lamar, featuring American singer Zacari. It was released as the third and 
  final single from Lamar's fourth studio album, "Damn" in 2017. The song, which is a 1980s-inspired pop rap and new-age 
  track, expresses Lamar's unconditional love for his significant other.The song charted in the top 40 in several countries and 
  reached number 11 on the Billboard Hot 100 in the United States. It also topped the R&B/Hip-Hop Airplay and Rhythmic 
  Airplay charts. The song has been certified four times platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA). 

  Songwriter(s):- Kendrick Duckworth, Zacari Pacaldo, Travis Walton, Mark Spears, Greg Kurstin, Anthony Tiffith.
  Producer(s):- Teddy Walton, Sounwave, Greg Kursti,Top Dawg.
