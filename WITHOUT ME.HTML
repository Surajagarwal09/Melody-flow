WIHTOUT ME


  "Without Me" is a song by American rapper Eminem from his fourth studio album The Eminem Show (2002). 
 "Without Me" was released on May 13, 2002, as the lead single from the album, and re-released on his greatest hits 
 compilation album Curtain Call: The Hits (2005). "Without Me" is one of Eminem's most successful singles, reaching 
 number two on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100, and number one in fifteen countries.

  Songwriter(s):- Marshall Mathers, Jeffrey Bass, Kevin Bell, Anne Dudley, Malcolm McLaren, Trevor Horn.
  Producer(s):- Eminem, Jeff Bass, DJ Head.
