ALL THE STARS


 "All the Stars" is a song by Kendrick Lamar and SZA, released as the lead single for the Black Panther film soundtrack in 2018. 
  The song was produced by Anthony "Top Dawg" Tiffith, Sounwave, and Al Shux. It was announced alongside the news that Lamar 
  and Tiffith would be producing the entire soundtrack, a decision made by the film's director, Ryan Coogler. The song played 
  during the film's end credits and received numerous accolades. It was nominated for Best Original Song at the 76th Golden Globe 
  Awards and the 91st Academy Awards, and received four nominations at the 61st Grammy Awards. It won Best Song at the 
  African-American Film Critics Association and its video won Best Visual Effects at the 2018 MTV Video Music Awards.
